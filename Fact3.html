Point of Interest: Las Vegas

Las Vegas is a city in Nevada notorious for gambling.
It's not out-of-the-ordinary during the day, but when night rolls around, a spectacular light show appears.
It is also home to very famous "Welcome to Las Vegas" sign, and the main street (called The Strip) has many notable places to go.
The most notable of these are the Stratosphere, Circus Circus, The Adventuredome, and The Luxor.
